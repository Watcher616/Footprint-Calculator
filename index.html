Summary

Your carbon footprint is a measure of the amount of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases that are released into the atmosphere as a result of your activities. By using this calculator, you can get an estimate of your carbon footprint based on your transportation and energy usage.

Once you know your carbon footprint, you can take steps to reduce it by making changes to your lifestyle and habits. Some simple steps you can take include walking or biking instead of driving, using public transportation, and reducing your energy usage at home.

By taking action to reduce your carbon footprint, you can help to mitigate the effects of climate change and contribute to a more sustainable future for our planet.
